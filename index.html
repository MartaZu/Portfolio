About

Business strategy
meets product design

Digital product designer and business strategist with over 7 years of experience designing digital solutions for enterprise clients across Energy, Financial Services, Automotive, and Public Administration.

I work at the intersection of business strategy and product design — leading end-to-end initiatives from vision definition to MVP validation. I coordinate multidisciplinary teams, translate commercial objectives into functional requirements, and shape design decisions on the basis of market evidence and user research.

Over the past few years I have integrated AI-assisted design and rapid prototyping into consulting workflows, significantly reducing the time between ideation and validation.
